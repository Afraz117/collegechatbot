CAMPUSCONNECT UNIVERSITY
OFFICIAL ACADEMIC REGULATIONS & ADMISSION GUIDELINES 2026-2027
=========================================

1. INTRODUCTION
Welcome to CampusConnect University. This document outlines the official policies for admission, eligibility, courses, fees, and campus regulations.

2. PROGRAMS OFFERED
We offer the following undergraduate programs:
- B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS)
- B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
- B.Tech in Electronics and Communication (ECE)
- Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
To be eligible for engineering programs (B.Tech), candidates must have:
- A minimum of 60% aggregate marks in 10+2 (HSC) or equivalent examination.
- Mandatory subjects in 10+2: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- For BBA, a minimum of 50% in any stream in 10+2 is required.

4. FEE STRUCTURE (Per Semester)
- B.Tech (CSE, AI & DS): $4,500
- B.Tech (ME, ECE): $4,000
- BBA: $3,000
- Hostel Accommodation: $1,200 per semester (includes meals)
- Library & Lab Fees: $200 per semester
Note: Scholarships up to 50% on tuition fees are available for students scoring above 90% in HSC.

5. ADMISSION TIMELINE & DEADLINES
- Application Start Date: January 15, 2026
- Application Deadline: May 30, 2026
- Entrance Exam (Online): June 15, 2026
- Merit List Declaration: July 1, 2026
- Last Date for Fee Payment: July 20, 2026
- Orientation and Classes Begin: August 10, 2026

6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
Q: Can I apply if my results are awaited?
A: Yes, you can apply with provisional results, but final admission requires submission of actual mark sheets before August 1st.

Q: Is hostel mandatory?
A: No, hostel accommodation is entirely optional. Day scholars can utilize college transport.

Q: Does the university offer placements?
A: Yes, our dedicated placement cell ensures 95% placement rates with top recruiters like Google, Amazon, IBM, and Microsoft.

Q: How do I contact the admission office?
A: You can reach us at admissions@campusconnect.edu or call 1-800-CAMPUS.

7. ATTENDANCE POLICY
A minimum of 75% attendance is strictly required in all theoretical and practical classes to be eligible to appear for the end-semester examinations.
